Purpose

I developed this site to show the power of FPGAs, particularly the MiSTer FPGA. I wanted to provide a visual representation of all the games that are currently playable. You will find title screens, in-game screens and long play videos. For right now, I'm focusing on Arcade games. I will gradually add more sections in the future for NES, SNES, Gameboy, GBA, Sega Master System, Game Gear, Sega Genesis, TurboGrafx-16 (PC Engine), Neo-Geo and more! This website is just informational, you will not find any roms.

Benefits of MiSTer FPGA
  • Extremely low audio/video latency on modern displays.
  • Games are incredibly responsive and feel just like they did when you were a kid. Your skills haven't diminished over time, you've just been playing with lag for the last 20+ years.
  • Use any USB controller or original console controllers through low-latency adapters.
  • Open-source.
  • Pick one up today at: https://misteraddons.com and https://www.misterkits.com
