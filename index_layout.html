Benefits MiSTer FPGA
  • Extremely low audio/video latency on modern displays.
  • Games are incredibly responsive and feel just like they did when you were a kid.
  • Your skills haven't diminished over time, you've just been playing with lag for the last 20+ years.
  • Use any USB controller or original console controllers through low-latency adapters.
  • Open-source.
  • Pick one up today at: https://misteraddons.com and https://www.misterkits.com
