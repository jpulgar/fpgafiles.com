Purpose

I developed this site to show the power of FPGAs, particularly the MiSTer FPGA. I wanted to show a visual representation of all the games that are playable through the MiSTer. For right now, I will concentrate on showing the supported Arcade games. But I will add more sections in the future for NES, SNES, Gameboy, GBA, Sega Master System, Game Gear, Sega Genesis, TurboGrafx-16 (PC Engine), Neo-Geo and more!

Benefits of MiSTer FPGA

  • Extremely low audio/video latency on modern displays.
  • Games are incredibly responsive and feel just like they did when you were a kid. Your skills haven't diminished over time, you've just been playing with lag for the last 20+ years.
  • Use any USB controller or original console controllers through low-latency adapters.
  • Open-source.
